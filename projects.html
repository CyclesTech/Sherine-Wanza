Projects & Research

Influence of Customer Service on Organizational Identity

Description: This research project examines how customer service practices impact an organization's public identity and reputation. The study explores the relationship between customer interactions and brand perception, providing insights into how effective customer service can enhance organizational identity and drive customer loyalty. The findings offer strategic recommendations for organizations looking to strengthen their brand through improved customer service.

Areas of Focus: Customer Service, Brand Management, Organizational Identity

Crisis Communication in the Digital Age

Description: This project explores the role of digital media in crisis communication, analyzing how organizations can effectively manage public relations during crises using modern communication tools. The study highlights best practices for engaging with the public during crises and provides a framework for developing robust crisis communication strategies.

Areas of Focus: Crisis Communication, Digital Media, Public Relations

The Role of Social Media in Shaping Public Opinion

Description: This project investigates the impact of social media on public opinion, focusing on how communication strategies on platforms like Twitter and Facebook influence public discourse. The research provides insights into the power of social media as a tool for shaping public opinion and offers guidance on how organizations can leverage social media to engage with their audiences effectively.

Areas of Focus: Social Media, Public Opinion, Communication Strategies
